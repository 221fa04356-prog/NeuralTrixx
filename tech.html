Technology Consultant

₹40,000 – ₹80,000 per month

Position
Technology Consultant
Location

Employment Type

About

We are seeking a motivated Technology Consultant who enjoys working closely with clients to solve real business challenges through technology. In this role, you will collaborate with stakeholders to understand organizational needs, shape effective technology strategies, and support the delivery of tailored digital solutions that drive efficiency and growth.

Responsibilities

As a Technology Consultant, you will engage with clients to analyze their business problems and technical requirements. You will be involved in designing technology-driven approaches that align with business goals and assist teams in implementing customized digital solutions. Your role will require continuous collaboration to ensure solutions are practical, scalable, and successfully executed.

Qualifications

The ideal candidate demonstrates strong problem-solving capabilities, communicates ideas clearly, and has hands-on experience in enterprise-level technology planning or solution delivery. A background that combines analytical thinking with practical implementation skills is highly valued.

Skills

This role requires expertise in technology advisory practices, experience working with digital solutions, and the ability to maintain effective client engagement throughout project lifecycles.

Compensation

This position offers a competitive monthly salary ranging between ₹40,000 and ₹80,000, depending on experience and skill level.
