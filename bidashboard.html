Frequently Asked Questions

BI dashboards are used to monitor key business metrics, track performance, visualize trends, and provide real-time insights that support faster and more informed decision-making across teams.

Dashboards are interactive and continuously updated, allowing users to filter, drill down, and explore data—while traditional reports are often static snapshots generated on a fixed schedule.

BI dashboards can integrate data from databases, spreadsheets, cloud platforms, APIs, CRM systems, ERP tools, and data warehouses—providing a unified view across multiple business systems.

Dashboards can be updated in real time, near real time, or on scheduled intervals such as hourly, daily, or weekly—depending on business needs and data availability.

Deliverables typically include interactive dashboards, KPI definitions, automated reports, data visualizations, documentation, and user guidance to ensure long-term usability and adoption.
